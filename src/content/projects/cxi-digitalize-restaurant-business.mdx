## CXI: Business Management CRM/Loyalty

CXI is a SaaS solution for brands improve their customer experience in industries: F&B, Spa, Clinic, Community ...
CXI provides a smart approach to keep customers with brands by powerful CDP. CXI offer blockchain solution for enterprise/big community launch their own token.

### My Responsibilities

- **Ticketing & Events Module:** Developed a robust ticketing and event management system within CXI, enabling businesses to seamlessly organize events, manage ticket sales and registration, and track attendee data.
- **Enhanced Authentication:** Expanded the platform's authentication capabilities by integrating Facebook login, Google login, and Firebase OTP (One-Time Password) authentication flows, providing users with more convenient and secure access options.
- **Automated Email Communication:** Implemented Laravel Commands and Jobs to automate personalized email communication with users, such as birthday greetings and targeted promotions, based on specific conditions and triggers.

### Features

- Generate an app easily with drag-n-drop UI
- CRM for contact management, case management, sale performance.
- Solutions for restaurants: POS, Kitchen display, Inventory, Delivery
- Solution for community: events, eCommerce, membership ...
