## Tan Hong My

Tan Hong My company is a leading beauty company in Ho Chi Minh City that is dedicated to providing top-quality beauty services and products.
I joined Tan Hong My as a key member of their newly formed IT department. Played a pivotal role in spearheading the development of custom software solutions to digitize and streamline their core business operations.

### My Responsibilities

- **Management Application:** Created a robust management application to centralize control over staff, clients, branches, inventory, products, services, and finances.
- **Staff Application:** Designed and built a user-friendly staff application that streamlined workflows for each department, fostering seamless collaboration and communication across the organization.
- **Client Application & Social Network:** Created a client-centric mobile application and integrated social network to enhance the customer experience. This platform showcased services, facilitated marketing campaigns, enabled easy appointment booking, and provided personalized post-procedure care.
- **Real-Time Communication:** Implemented real-time chat features to enable direct communication between staff members, as well as between staff and clients. Integrated the chat system with Facebook pages to consolidate messaging channels and simplify customer interactions.
- **Third-party Integration:** Successfully integrated with third-party providers to enable in-app calling and video calling capabilities, further enhancing communication and convenience for both staff and clients.
- **Collaborator Management Platform:** Developed a robust platform to manage relationships with collaborators, track commissions, and generate insightful reports. This tool streamlined administrative tasks and provided valuable insights into the company's partnerships.
