Joined Tan Hong My, a leading beauty company in Ho Chi Minh City, as a key member of their newly established IT department. Spearheaded the development of custom software solutions aimed at digitizing and optimizing their business processes.

### My Tasks

- **Management Application**: Created a robust management application to centralize control over staff, clients, branches, inventory, products, services, and finances.
- **Staff Application**: Designed a user-friendly staff application with clearly defined workflows for each department, facilitating efficient inter-departmental collaboration.
- **Client Application & Social Network**: Built a client-facing application and social network to showcase services, run marketing campaigns, enable easy booking, and provide post-procedure care, thereby elevating the customer experience.
- **Real-time Chat**: Implemented real-time chat functionality between staff members and between staff and clients, integrating with Facebook pages for streamlined message management.
- **Third-party Integration**: Integrated with third-party providers to enable app-to-app and app-to-phone number calling/video call capabilities.
- Developed and implemented a comprehensive **collaborator management platform** to streamline team management, commission tracking, reporting.
