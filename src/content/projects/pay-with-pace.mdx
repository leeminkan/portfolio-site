## Pay With Pace

Pay With Pace is a flexible payment solution that empowers customers to split online purchases into three equal, interest-free installments using the Pace Card. With no hidden fees, the Pace Card is designed to make shopping more accessible and affordable across a wide range of brands.

### My Responsibilities

- **Marketing Website Development:** Built engaging marketing websites for Pace, including landing pages and campaign-specific pages, to attract and educate potential customers.
- **E-commerce Platform Integrations:**
  - Collaborated with various e-commerce platforms (Shopify, WooCommerce, OpenCart, Magento, etc.) to develop customized widgets and plugins.
  - These integrations enable merchants to seamlessly offer Pace's "buy now, pay later" option to their customers during checkout.
- **Merchant Support:**
  - Provided technical assistance and troubleshooting to merchants encountering integration issues.
  - Ensured a smooth and successful integration process for merchants of all sizes and technical backgrounds.
